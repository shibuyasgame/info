Pins

General Tidbits:
・ A deck in battle can have MAX 6 pins.
・ By default, all pins have a cooldown of 1 turn unless otherwise specified.
・ Cooldown begin after the effect's fully ended.
・ Knockback and Stun pins have a cooldown of 2 turns, MAX 1 in a deck.
・ Poison and HP drain effect pins have a cooldown of 2 turns.
・ Multi-hit pins have a cooldown of 2 turns, MAX 2 in a deck.
・ Pins with the potential to hit more than once per turn are considered multi-hit.
・ By default, healing pins can only be used once per battle unless otherwise specified, MAX 1 in a deck.
・ Splish Splash Barrier is a healing pin; Creepy Weepy is not.
・ Information about fusions can be found here.

Evolution Information:
Pin evolution is priced by the new attack (ususally the max potential damage per turn) of the pin will evolve into. It does not take into consideration your augmented stats, merely the pin's base stats.

・ If the new pin’s max damage is between 1-30, it will cost 80PP.
・ For pins between 31-42, it will cost 120PP, or 50 RP.
・ For pins between 43-50, it will cost 200PP, or 50 RP.
・ For pins above 51, it will cost 300PP, or 75RP.
・ Any heal or status pins will evolve for 120PP/50RP if they have no base attack.
・ The charge-line pins PP are calculated by their base attack alone, not their max potential damage.
・ Pins that both attack and inflict statuses' PP are calculated by x1.5 their base attack.
